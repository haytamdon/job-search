# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Job seekers in software engineering, AI, ML, and data science looking to relocate to countries such as Germany or the United Kingdom who require visa sponsorship. They need to rapidly scan the market, track their searches, and evaluate job details with maximum clarity and efficiency.

## Product Purpose
To automate LinkedIn job scraping for tech roles with visa relocation support and display results inside a clean, high-contrast dashboard with task progress monitoring and persistence.

## Brand Personality
Minimalist, high-contrast, utility-driven, and highly readable. Focuses on structural grid-line layout, clean type hierarchy, low cognitive load, and clean developer-tool aesthetic in light mode.

## Anti-references
Overly flashy/playful designs, excessive bounce animations, sketchy SVG illustrations, multi-color gradient text, over-rounded borders, and generic dark-mode SaaS dashboard clichés.

## Design Principles
1. **Utility Over Decoration**: Spacing, layout, and visual indicators serve information hierarchy rather than pure visual flair.
2. **Extreme Readability**: High-contrast type on clean light-mode surfaces to ensure rapid data scanning.
3. **Structured Alignment**: Emphasize clean, thin borders and structural grid lines to organize components.
4. **Resilient Feedback**: Real-time progress trackers and health monitors provide clear, non-distracting status updates.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict WCAG AA contrast (≥4.5:1 for body and placeholder copy).
- Support for system reduced-motion preferences.
- Screen-reader accessible table layouts and buttons.
